About

About Me

I am a researcher with training in biotechnology, neuroimaging, and computational analysis. My work integrates statistical genetics, machine learning, imaging, and omics approaches to study aging and age-related brain disorders. I am particularly interested in understanding how vascular injury, molecular pathways, and peripheral organ systems contribute to dementia risk and progression.

My projects span tissue- and cell-specific heritability analysis, disease progression modeling, spatial transcriptomics, automated image segmentation, and natural language processing of clinical radiology reports. I enjoy building reproducible pipelines that connect biological questions to clinically meaningful insights.

Research

Research Interests

Neuroimaging & Brain Aging

Investigating white matter hyperintensities, vascular brain injury, choroid plexus alterations, and imaging-based markers of neurodegeneration across aging and dementia.

Statistical Genetics

Using LDSC, MAGMA, FUSION, and related approaches to identify tissue- and cell-specific genetic enrichment patterns and shared mechanisms across WMHs and Alzheimer’s disease.

Spatial & Molecular Omics

Integrating spatial transcriptomics, antibody capture data, and molecular profiling to characterize tissue states, immune infiltration, and disease-associated cellular programs.

Machine Learning for Medicine

Applying deep learning and progression modeling to segmentation, patient subtyping, radiology-report classification, and biomarker prediction.

Brain-Body Interactions

Exploring how systemic organ systems such as liver, kidney, heart, and vasculature relate to brain pathology and neurodegenerative disease.

Translational Biomarkers

Identifying clinically relevant biomarkers and therapeutic targets by integrating imaging, genetics, proteomics, and computational modeling.

Projects

Selected Projects

Brain-Body Axis of White Matter Hyperintensities

Characterizing tissue- and cell-specific partitioned heritability of white matter hyperintensities and identifying shared genetic architecture with Alzheimer’s disease and related dementias.

LDSC · MAGMA · FUSION · HPA · STRING

SuStaIn Modeling of WMH Topography

Applying subtype and stage inference to arterial-territory and lobar WMH burden to identify progression patterns, disease subtypes, hemispheric asymmetry, and clinical associations.

SuStaIn · Python · Neuroimaging · Disease Progression

Spatial Transcriptomics in Myocarditis

Analyzing Visium spatial transcriptomics and antibody capture data to map immune cell distributions, tissue states, and spatially localized inflammatory programs in myocarditis.

Seurat · semla · Visium · Spatial Omics

Neu-RadBERT for Radiology Report Classification

Building NLP pipelines to classify non-mutually exclusive brain abnormalities from free-text radiology reports using transformer-based language models.

Transformers · NLP · Radiology · Clinical AI

ASCHOPLEX Fine-Tuning for Choroid Plexus Segmentation

Evaluating and fine-tuning deep-learning-based segmentation of the choroid plexus in multi-center neurodegenerative cohorts, with benchmarking against expert manual masks.

Deep Learning · MRI · Segmentation · Clinical Imaging

Multi-Omic Biomarker Discovery

Integrating metabolomics, imaging, genetics, and proteomic evidence to identify biomarkers linked to healthy aging, dementia, and vascular injury.

Metabolomics · Biomarkers · Omics Integration
